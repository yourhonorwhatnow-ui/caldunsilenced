1. What This Website Is

CALD Unsilenced is a free community information website. Our purpose is to:

  • Provide general legal information and direct people to the right support organisations
  • Share criminology and science articles relevant to domestic violence and justice
  • Publish poetry and creative writing from and for the CALD community
  • Point people toward professional help — we are a signpost, not a service provider
💡 We do not collect personal information, provide legal advice, or act as a counselling service. For professional help please contact the organisations listed on our website.

2. No Personal Information Collected

We want to be completely clear — CALD Unsilenced does not collect, store, or use your personal information.

  • No registration or account is required to use this website
  • We do not have contact forms that collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not run mailing lists or newsletters
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising technology
  • We do not know who visits our website

You can read, browse, and use every part of this website completely anonymously. Your safety and privacy are our priority.

💡 If you are in a dangerous situation and worried about your browsing history, remember you can use your browser's private/incognito mode to visit this website without it appearing in your history.

3. Submitting Poetry & Creative Writing

We welcome poetry and creative writing submissions from CALD women and community members. Submissions are entirely anonymous — we do not need or want your real name.

✍️ How to submit anonymously

Simply send your poem or writing with either:

"Anonymous" — or — your initials only (e.g. "A.M.")

That is all we will publish. No name, no location, no identifying information.

By submitting your writing you confirm that:

  • The work is your own original creation
  • You give us permission to publish it on this website
  • You understand it will be published under "Anonymous" or your initials only
  • You retain full ownership and copyright of your work

We reserve the right to decline submissions that contain identifying information about other individuals, or content that could cause harm.
