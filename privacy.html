Privacy
Policy

Short version: we don't collect your information. Full version below.

We do not collect
your personal information.

CALD Unsilenced is a free information website. You do not need to register, log in, or share any personal details to use this website. Everything is completely anonymous.

What We Collect — Nothing

We want to be completely transparent:

  • No registration or login is required
  • No contact forms collect your name, email, or phone number
  • No mailing lists or newsletters
  • No advertising or tracking technology
  • No cookies that identify you personally
  • We do not know who visits our website
💡 You can read every single page of this website without us knowing anything about you. That is intentional — your safety matters more than our analytics.

Browsing Safely

If you are in a dangerous situation and worried about someone seeing that you visited this website, you can protect yourself by using your browser's private or incognito mode.

💡 How to browse privately:
On Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+N (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+N (Mac)
On Safari: File → New Private Window
On Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+P (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+P (Mac)
This means your visit will not appear in your browsing history.

Poetry Submissions

If you submit a poem or piece of writing to us, we publish it either as "Anonymous" or with initials only (e.g. "A.M."). We do not ask for or publish your name, location, or any identifying information.

You retain full ownership and copyright of any writing you submit. We simply display it on our website with your permission.

External Links

Our website links to external organisations such as Victoria Legal Aid, Safe Steps, inTouch, and 1800RESPECT. When you click those links you leave our website and their own privacy policies apply.

We do not share any information about you with those organisations — because we don't have any information about you to share.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how this website works in a way that affects privacy — for example if we add a contact form or submission function in the future — we will update this policy and clearly explain what information is collected and why.
